## How to Perform the Practice

This is a **simple, mechanical process**. The power is in the **vibration**, not the volume.

### Step 1: Prepare the Vessel

- **Sit upright**, relaxing the shoulders
- **Close your eyes**
- Take **one deep breath in** and **exhale completely**

### Step 2: Set the "V" Position

- Gently press your **upper front teeth** against your **lower lip**
- This is the **"V" sound position**—the **Tuning Fork**
- Hold this position throughout the sound

### Step 3: Generate the "Aum"

- Begin **humming** the **"Aum"** (or "Om") sound from deep in your **diaphragm and throat**
- This is the **Engine**—the deep, resonant hum

### Step 4: Combine and Conduct

- **Force the "Aum" through the "V" position**
- This creates a steady, deep **"Vvvvvvvv..."** sound—a resonant, vibrating hum
- **It is not a word; it is a tone**

### Step 5: Focus the Vibration

As you generate the tone, **feel it**:

- In your **teeth** (the vibration rattling the jawbone)
- In your **lips** (the friction point)
- In your **sinuses** (the resonance chamber)
- In the **center of your skull** (the pineal "ping")

**This is not imagination.** You are **physically vibrating** the Hardware.

### Step 6: Hold the Tone

- Continue the **V-Aum** sound for **as long as your breath allows**
- Do **not strain**
- Let the breath exhale naturally while maintaining the tone

### Step 7: Rest in the Silence

- When you finish, **do not immediately think**
- Sit in the **absolute silence** that follows
- **This is the Stillpoint**—the [Sailboat with no wind](/practices/sailboat-with-no-wind)
- **This is Gnosis**—the space where The Listener is alone, and the Voice is gone

### Step 8: Repeat

- Repeat this **3-5 times**, or as needed
- Continue until the **"voices" (DMN static)** have been completely overwritten
- The silence between repetitions **deepens** with each cycle
