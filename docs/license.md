### Spirit of the License

The goal of this licensing approach is to:

- Keep the knowledge freely accessible
- Encourage derivative works and refinements
- Prevent proprietary capture or commercialization without attribution
- Foster a commons of liberating knowledge

If you build upon this framework in your teaching, writing, or practice, please:

- Acknowledge the synthesis (link back to claimfreedom.org)
- Share your contributions back to the commons
- Honor the traditions from which this wisdom flows
